# Transformers

Transformers are a neural network architecture that uses attention mechanisms for sequence modeling. They replaced RNNs for most NLP tasks.

Key components include self-attention with queries, keys, and values, multi-head attention, feed-forward layers, and positional encoding.

The Co-Wiki uses TF-IDF over transformer-related content to build initial activation vectors. See [[ai/spreading-activation]] for how those vectors propagate through the knowledge graph.
